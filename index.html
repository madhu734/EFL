About EFL
The Tournament

The English Football League (EFL) is the annual intra-departmental football tournament organized by students of the Department of English, Jahangirnagar University. Now entering its 5th season, EFL has grown into a celebrated tradition that brings together football enthusiasts from all batches.

Tournament Rules
01
Tournament Format:
In the primary group stage, all teams will play against each other. The top 4 teams on the points table will advance. Semi-final 1: 1st vs 4th. Semi-final 2: 2nd vs 3rd. The winners will play the Final.
02
Match Duration:
Each match consists of 30 minutes (15 minutes per half) with a 10-minute half-time break.
03
Walkover & Forfeit:
Teams must be present at the Central Field at the scheduled time. If one team fails to appear, the opponent gets a 2-0 win. If both teams fail to appear, both will be considered to have lost by 1 goal.
04
Knockout Ties:
If a knockout match ends in a draw, the winner will be decided directly through a penalty shootout tiebreaker.
